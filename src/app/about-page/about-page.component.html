Organize your job hunt

Gig Journal takes the stress out of keeping track of your professional endevours.

Applications

Anyone who is looking for jobs in today's market knows that the amount of jobs you apply for can get quickly out of hand. With Gig Journal, you can keep track of what jobs you have applied for. In each job, you can have a list of tasks you need to complete (For example "Follow up on March 31st with Hiring Manager"), keep notes on important events like interviews, and have a list of contacts to keep your leads organized.

Freelancers

In the "gig economy" of today, freelancers will quickly accumulate a handful of clients. Instead of having each client's info scattered accross notebooks, phone contacts, and email contacts, keep track of your professional life with Gig Journal. You can create seperate modules for each client, with tasks you may need to complete, important events, and contacts.

Professionals

Even if you aren't actively seeking a new job, Gig Journal can help you organize your professional network. Keep an entry for all of the businesses/clients you deal with, and no longer will you be scrambling for contact info when it comes time to reach a past client.
